Vacation and Excursion Planner

The VEP app will have a simple and efficient interface. When entering the app customers of WWV will be able to add a new vacation and enter details such as date, name, and lodging. While creating a vacation or later on, the customer will be able to add excursions to their vacation. Excursion details include date and the name of the excursion. Customers can receive notifications of their vacations and excursions. Customers will also be able to quickly find information about their vacation packages using a built in search function. Finally, if customers wish to, they can generate a report of their vacations and excursions to keep separately or print. This will help resolve the first need of WWV, which is to reduce the extra volume of calls that the customer service team is receiving. This is because customers are calling to receive information about their vacation package and now can enter it into the app instead. The app will also help resolve the second issue that WWV is having. That is that customers often complain that they miss excursions because of losing track or forgetting. The app will have the option to notify the customers so they don’t lose track.

User Guide

Introduction

This guide will demonstrate how to install, log into, register, and use all of the functions of the Vacation and Excursion Planner application.

Installing the Application

1. On the device that you would like to install the app, open your preferred web browser.

2. In the web browser go to https://lhartw7.github.io/D424-Vacation-and-Excursion-Planner/

3. Click the download link under “Download Here”.

4. Open to your device’s file management app, usually “Files by Google” or “My Files”.

5. Open the downloads folder and click the vacation-and-excursion-planner-x.x.apk

6. You will be prompted by the device asking if you would like to install the app, select yes.

7. You will receive a notification when the app has finished installing.

Registering and Logging In

1. To register or log in, you will first need to open the VEP app by selecting the newly installed icon.

2. If it’s your first time using the app, you will need to register. Specify a username and strong password. The password must be 14 characters, contain a letter, and contain a number.

3. Select the register button to register your account and be logged in for the first time.

4. If you’re already registered, enter your username, password, and select login.


Vacations

All the following require you to be logged into the app. See the registering and logging in section. When you first log in you will be on the vacations report screen. By default, this report will display all of your vacations, the start, and end date.

Create Vacation

1. On the vacations report screen, select the + at the bottom right of the screen.

2. In the vacation details, specify the title, your hotel, the start date, and the end date.

3. Press the 3 dots at the top right to open the drop down and select save.

Delete Vacation

Before you can delete a vacation, you must delete all attached excursions. Excursions are listed in the vacation details screen.

1. On the vacations report screen, select the vacation you would like to delete by tapping it.

2. Press the 3 dots at the top right of the screen to open the drop-down menu and select delete.

Modify Vacation

1. On the vacations report screen, select the vacation you would like to modify by tapping it.

2. Modify any of the fields you would like to change.

3. Press the 3 dots at the top right of the screen to open the drop-down menu and select save.

Creating a Report

1. The vacations report screen by default generates a report with every vacation, the start date, and the end date.

2. Modify the report by selecting the search icon and typing what you would like to include in your report.

3. The results will be automatically generated below the search bar.


Excursions

Create Excursion

1. On the vacations report screen, select the vacation you would like to add an excursion to by tapping it.

2. In the bottom right of the vacation details screen, select the + button.

3. Specify the title and date of your excursion in the excursion details section.

4. Press the 3 dots at the top right of the screen to open the drop-down menu and select save.

Delete Excursion

1. Select the vacation that you would like to delete an excursion from on the vacations report screen.

2. Select the excursion you would like to delete by tapping it on the vacation details screen.

3. Press the 3 dots at the top right of the screen to open the drop-down menu and select delete.

Modify Excursion

1. Select the vacation that you would like to modify an excursion of on the vacations report screen.

2. Select the excursion you would like to modify by tapping it on the vacation details screen.

3. Modify any field you would like to change.

4. Press the 3 dots at the top of the screen to open the drop-down menu and select save.

Receiving Alerts

1. To receive alerts on the start date of your vacations and excursions, first select the vacation or excursion you want to receive a notification for.

2. Press the 3 dots at the top right of the screen to open the drop down menu and select Alert.

3. You will now receive a notification on the date of the vacation or excursion.

Sharing Vacations

1. To share a vacation and the related excursions, first select a vacation from the vacations report screen.

2. Press the 3 dots at the top right corner of the screen to open the drop-down menu and select Share.

3. Select one of the options to where you would like to share your message, such as messaging or email.
